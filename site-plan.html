Overview

Site Name: ServiceHub

I chose this site name because of the idea of making meal planning and cooking more accessible and fun.

Domain name: shub.com

Site Purpose

A digital recipe book that allows users to browse, view, and contribute recipes. This website aims to provide a space where users can: Find new and exciting meal ideas, Learn how to cook a variety of meals, Share their own favorite recipes with others, Organize meals by category, preference, or cooking time.
